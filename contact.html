Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Lopez Productions.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple brand kits take 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive automation systems can take 4-6 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline in your proposal.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide. All communication and project management happens remotely, so location isn't a barrier.

What if I need changes after the project is complete?

I include 30 days of support after project completion for any adjustments or questions. Additional support and modifications can be arranged.

Do you provide training on the systems you build?

Absolutely! Every project includes training and documentation so you can confidently use and maintain your new systems.

Can you work with my existing tools and systems?

Yes! I'll work with your current tools whenever possible and only recommend new ones if they'll significantly improve your workflow.

What makes you different from other automation specialists?

I focus specifically on non-technical users and practical solutions. I speak your language, not tech jargon, and build systems that actually work in the real world.

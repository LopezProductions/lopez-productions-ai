Featured Case Study: Content Creator Automation Suite

How I helped a YouTube creator save 20+ hours per week and double their content output.

The Challenge

A growing YouTube creator was spending 30+ hours per week on repetitive tasks like thumbnail creation, video scheduling, and analytics tracking. They wanted to focus more on content creation and less on administrative work.

The Solution

I built a comprehensive automation suite using Zapier, Notion, and custom integrations that handles:

  • Automatic thumbnail generation using Canva API
  • Video scheduling across multiple platforms
